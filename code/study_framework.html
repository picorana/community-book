Study on Multivariate Network Visualization

Please read the information and consent to participate in this study.

We appreciate your input and record your responses anonymously!

Read the introduction below to learn about the network you will be interacting with.

In general, a network consists of nodes. These are connected by edges.

For this study, we use randomly generated networks of friendships. Each person is represented as a node.

The friendship between two nodes is indicated by an edge.

Further, a friendship can be described by the following attributes: Years of Friendship, Distance, Interactions per Week, and Common Hobbies. This makes the network multivariate.

Structure of the Study

This study is divided into 3 task sessions.

Before each session, you will get familiar with the task type by trying to solve 3 training tasks.

To be fully compensated for this study, you must solve at least 2 of 3 tasks at the start of each session!

After the training, you are asked to solve 9 tasks of the same type. They are presented in increasing order of difficulty.

In each task, you are asked to select nodes. This can be done by click. A node can be unselected by another click. You can clear your whole answer using the button Clear.

If you are satisfied with your answer, click on the button Check to get feedback in the training session or Submit.

Apart from the accuracy of your solution, your completion time will be measured for each task.

However, you are allowed to rest after each task or session before continuing by clicking on Next.

The study should take approximately 50 minutes to complete.
